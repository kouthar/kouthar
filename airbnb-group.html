Airbnb Group Booking: Starting a Group

from Artiom Dashinsky

Still in development for mobile.

Time: March 2020 - Present
Skills: Design Thinking, UI Design, Wireframing, Prototyping
Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Figma, Procreate

Problem Statement

"Design a feature for Airbnb, allowing a group of friends to plan and book their mutual vacation."

Understanding the Problem & Brainstorming

5 W's and 1 H

Designing the Solution

While working through the problem, I soon realised that there were three main parts users go through when booking a trip - starting a group, browsing for places to stay, then deciding on a place and booking, each with different user needs and requirements.

I decided to focus on designing the user experience for starting a group.

Starting a Group

After brainstorming and mapping, I then able to turn my flows into mockups!

Under the assumption that group booking is a relevant enough function to be its own feature, I designed a very similar user flow to inputting guests and filters for cohesion and to ensure an uninterrupted, smooth user experience.

Saved groups would show up under the "Saved" section, as do all others do, with saving and transaction flow to be similar to the current Airbnb user flow for a consistent user experience.
